Chandigarh University,panjab

Chandigarh University is a private university located in Mohali, Punjab, India. The university was established in 2012 by the Punjab State Legislature and is recognized by University Grants Commission under Section 2 with the right to confer degrees as per Section 22 of the UGC Act, 1956.
